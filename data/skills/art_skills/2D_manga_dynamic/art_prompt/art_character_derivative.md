Character variants derived from the base designs in art_character.md. Reference art_character.md for the core character templates.

### Variant Types

**Outfit Variants**
- Casual modern streetwear: hoodies, sneakers, layered jackets with kanji prints
- Traditional garb: hakama, haori, kimono with clan crests, geta sandals
- Training gear: gi with torn sleeves, wrapped hands, sweat details
- Formal uniform: high-collared jackets, insignia patches, polished boots
- Seasonal variations: summer (light fabrics), winter (heavy coats, scarves, breath visible)

**Expression Variants**
- Extended emotion range beyond the base 16-panel grid
- Subtle micro-expressions: quirked eyebrow, twitching eye, clenched jaw
- Action expressions: wind-blown hair, sweat drops, vein marks, shadowed eyes
- Chibi/deformed expressions for comedic beats
- Silent panel expressions: wordless close-ups conveying complex emotion through eyes alone

**Battle-Damaged State**
- Torn clothing with ragged edge line-art
- Bruises rendered with screentone gradients
- Blood spatter: directional splashes following impact trajectory
- Cracked armor with exposed under-layer
- Exhausted stance: slumped shoulders, heavy breathing indicators, sweat trails
- Wrapped bandages with visible blood seepage
- Scar accumulation showing fight progression
- Weapon damage: chipped blades, frayed handles, cracked hilts

**Power-Up / Transformation States**
- Aura activation stages: dormant → flickering → full blaze
- Hair color shift gradient from base to powered
- Clothing energy infusion: fabric edges glowing with inner light
- Tattoo/marking emergence: glowing patterns spreading across skin
- Eye transformation: pupil shape change, iris color shift, sclera darkening
- Silhouette-only frame for maximum impact reveal moment

Manga/anime character design. Templates for:

### (1) Battle Key-Visual
Two fighters mid-clash with signature jutsu/techniques colliding. Shockwave emanating from point of impact. Dynamic perspective with extreme foreshortening — one fighter lunging toward camera, the other bracing. Speed lines radiating outward from the collision center. Energy auras rendered with cel-shaded light blooms. Debris particles suspended in mid-air. Deep navy background with electric cyan energy streaks. Crimson accent lighting on character silhouettes. Heavy rim-light separating fighters from background. Motion-blur trails on limbs and weapons.

### (2) Character Cast Sheet
2×5 ten-panel grid. Each panel features a distinct character archetype. Consistent art direction across all panels — same line quality, same cel-shading thickness, same color saturation level. Pastel accents on character accessories (hair clips, belts, weapon grips). Name tags in bottom corner of each panel in clean sans-serif font. Front-facing or three-quarter poses. Simple gradient background behind each character. Height chart reference line at bottom of sheet. Color palette swatches panel in top-left corner of sheet.

### (3) Expression Grid
16-panel face grid (4×4). Single character, consistent facial features across all panels — same eye shape, nose placement, jaw line. Expressions covered: neutral, joy, rage, sorrow, surprise, fear, disgust, determination, smugness, embarrassment, exhaustion, maniacal laughter, cold glare, hopeful smile, battle cry, peaceful calm. Clean line-art with minimal shading for readability. Consistent lighting direction from upper-left. Head-and-shoulders crop for each panel. Expression label text below each panel in small font.

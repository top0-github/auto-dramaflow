### Template: Weapons

- Speed-line emphasis along the blade edge — multiple thin parallel lines following the cutting arc
- Screentone shading on the blade flat to indicate metallic curvature

- Impact marks: chipped edge details, cracked blade patterns, dried blood residue
- Sheath/scabbard with lacquer-gradient rendering and sageo cord details
- Weapon rack display: multiple weapons arranged with consistent lighting and perspective

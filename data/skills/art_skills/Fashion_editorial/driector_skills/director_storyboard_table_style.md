# Director Storyboard Table Style — Fashion Editorial

## Editorial Spread Rhythm

A fashion editorial "story" typically spans 8-12 images across a magazine spread. The rhythm follows:

1. **Opener (Full Bleed)**: wide establishing shot, sets location and mood. Double-page spread. No text overlay.
2. **Detail / Accessory**: tight product shot, luxury item in focus. Single page.
3. **Full-Length Look**: model in full outfit, environmental context. Full page or double.
4. **Beauty Close-Up**: 85mm portrait, emotional connection. Single page.
5. **Action / Motion**: walking shot, fabric in motion. Double page.
6. **Silhouette / Backlit**: dramatic contrast, editorial gravitas. Single page.
7. **Grouping / Layering**: multiple pieces from the collection, styled together. Full page.
8. **Closer**: iconic single image, strong composition. Full bleed, sometimes with title overlay.

## Color Palette Locking

Each editorial "story" commits to one dominant palette. Do not mix palettes within a story.

| Editorial Style | Primary Palette | Accent | Mood |
|----------------|-----------------|--------|------|
| Urban Streetwear | Concrete gray, neon cyan, safety orange | Magenta | Gritty, electric |
| Haute Couture Runway | Stark white, charcoal, cold platinum | Crimson | Severe, architectural |
| Old Money Quiet Luxury | Camel, cream, forest green, burgundy | Gold | Warm, heritage |
| Y2K Revival | Hot pink, baby blue, chrome silver | Lime green | Nostalgic, playful |
| Avant-Garde Organic | Indigo, bone white, bioluminescent teal | Amber | Surreal, elemental |

## Typography Integration

### Principles
- Clean sans-serif for modern editorials (Helvetica Neue, Futura, or custom geometric sans)
- Embossed text treatment for luxury spreads (blind deboss on matte stock)
- Minimal text: titles only on opener/closer, no body copy on images
- Magazine masthead top-left or centered at top, small point size
- Credits block bottom-right, 6-8pt, 50% opacity

### Placement Rules
- Never place type over the model's face
- Type over negative space only (sky, solid wall, shadow area)
- Full-bleed images get floating type; bordered images get anchored type
- Runway credits: season, house name, look number — bottom margin, small caps

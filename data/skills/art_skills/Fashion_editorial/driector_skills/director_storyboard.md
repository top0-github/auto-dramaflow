# Director Storyboard — Fashion Editorial

## Fashion Prompt Structure

Build prompts in this order:
1. **Genre + Vibe**: e.g., "High-end fashion editorial, urban streetwear aesthetic"
2. **Model Description**: pose, expression, styling direction
3. **Garment Focus**: key piece, fabric behavior, silhouette
4. **Location + Lighting**: scene, key/fill/rim setup, time of day
5. **Camera + Film**: focal length, aperture, film stock simulation
6. **Post-Production Notes**: color grading, grain, retouching level

## Model Pose Vocabulary

### Standing / Walking
- Relaxed stance: weight on back leg, contrapposto, hands in pockets
- Dynamic stride: mid-step, coat billowing, purposeful direction
- Leaning: against brutalist wall, vintage car, or street lamp

### Editorial / Sculptural
- Sculptural stillness: angular limbs, mannequin-like, high-fashion frozen
- Seated power: low angle, legs crossed, direct eye contact
- Reclining: chaise longue or desert sand, languid, editorial spread

### Detail / Close-Up
- Beauty close-up: 85mm, eyes in sharp focus, skin texture visible
- Hands + accessories: nails, rings, clutch bags, precise DOF falloff
- Fabric macro: weave, drape, light transmission through sheer layers

## Fabric Behavior Keywords

### Drape
- Liquid drape: silk charmeuse, satin, bias-cut
- Structured drape: heavy wool, bonded neoprene, scuba
- Architectural drape: origami folds, sculptural organza, stiff taffeta

### Flow
- Wind flow: chiffon caught in breeze, directional movement
- Gravity flow: cascading tulle, waterfall ruffles
- Motion flow: walking-induced swirl, train trailing

### Structure
- Tailored structure: sharp shoulders, corseted waist, architectural seams
- Voluminous structure: ball gown, puffer, exaggerated proportions
- Minimal structure: slip dress, clean lines, Japanese simplicity

### Transparency
- Sheer overlay: organza over solid, layered translucency
- Lace transparency: intricate cutouts, skin reveal
- Wet transparency: rain-soaked fabric, cling, reflective sheen

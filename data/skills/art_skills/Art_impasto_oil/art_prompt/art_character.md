# 厚涂油画角色提示词

## 风格编号：No. 136（厚涂花卉人物风格）& No. 140（里维拉壁画工人风格）

## 基础提示词模板

```
A figure rendered in thick impasto oil paint, palette knife strokes creating 3D texture. [Description]. The paint is applied in heavy dollops and rhythmic swirls. Harsh [direction] light creates deep shadows within the paint ridges. Color palette: [specific colors]. Canvas texture visible. In the tradition of [post-impressionist/social realist] oil painting.
```

## 模板参数说明

| 参数 | 说明 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| [Description] | 角色外貌、姿态、服饰描述 | A woman in a flowing blue dress standing by a window |
| [direction] | 光源方向 | overhead midday / diagonal from left / frontal golden hour |
| [specific colors] | 具体颜料名称 | Chrome Yellow, Deep Ultramarine, Vermilion Red, Viridian Green |
| [tradition] | 油画传统流派 | post-impressionist / social realist |

## 后印象派厚涂角色（No. 136）

```
A figure rendered in thick impasto oil paint, palette knife strokes creating 3D texture. A woman in a flowing dress stands amidst a garden of sunflowers. The paint is applied in heavy dollops and rhythmic swirls, each fold of fabric built from overlapping palette knife ridges. Harsh midday sunlight creates deep shadows within the paint ridges. Color palette: Chrome Yellow dominating the sunflowers, Deep Ultramarine in the shadow folds, Vermilion Red accents in her lips and floral centers, Viridian Green in the stems and leaves. Canvas texture visible beneath the paint layers. In the tradition of post-impressionist oil painting, reminiscent of Van Gogh's expressive impasto technique.
```

## 社会现实主义壁画角色（No. 140）

```
A figure rendered in thick impasto oil paint, palette knife strokes creating 3D texture. A muscular worker leans on a hammer, sweat glistening on his brow, surrounded by the glow of a forge. The paint is applied in heavy dollops and rhythmic swirls, the musculature built up with sculptural palette knife strokes. Golden crucible light from below creates deep shadows within the paint ridges. Color palette: Burnt Sienna and Yellow Ochre for skin tones, Deep Ultramarine for work clothes, Vermilion Red for the forge glow, Chrome Yellow for the molten metal highlights. Canvas texture visible. In the tradition of social realist oil painting, reminiscent of Diego Rivera's mural workers.
```

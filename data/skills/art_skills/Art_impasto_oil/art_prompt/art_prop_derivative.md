# 厚涂油画道具衍生风格提示词

基于 `art_prop.md` 中的道具设定，在不同油画技法下的变体。

## 印象派断色道具

将道具以印象派短笔触技法呈现，色彩通过视觉混合而非物理调和。参考 `art_character_derivative.md` 中的印象派章节获取完整色彩方案。

```
A [prop] painted in Impressionist broken color technique. Short horizontal dabs of pure pigment placed side by side. [Prop texture details] suggested rather than sculpturally defined—edges soft, forms dissolving into the atmospheric light. No palette knife ridges; the surface is flickering and luminous. Color palette: [Lavender, Pale Peach, Sage Green, or adapted from parent prop]. Against a muted background with visible canvas weave.
```

## 霍克尼式平面丙烯道具

将道具以扁平化、高饱和度的霍克尼式丙烯技法呈现。

```
A [prop] painted in flat acrylic style. Clean unmodulated color planes with crisp graphic contours. [Prop details] reduced to essential shapes—no impasto texture, no atmospheric blending. Strong shadow shapes rendered as flat blocks of Turquoise or Ultramarine. Color palette: Turquoise Blue, Terracotta, Lemon Yellow, Emerald Green. Against a flat color field background with visible canvas weave.
```

## 里维拉式社会现实主义道具

将道具以纪念碑式的社会现实主义壁画技法呈现，强调体积感和质朴的泥土色调。

```
A [prop] painted in the monumental mural style of Diego Rivera. Broad, deliberate strokes build sculptural volume. [Prop details] rendered with weight and dignity—tools, vessels, and everyday objects treated as heroic subjects. Earthy palette: Burnt Sienna, Yellow Ochre, Terra Rosa, Raw Umber. Golden light wraps the form. Against a warm umber background with visible canvas weave.
```

## 混合与匹配说明

- 当场景中同时出现道具和角色时，保持道具与角色使用同一种油画技法
- 如需风格对比（如同一个物件在不同艺术家笔下的演绎），参考本文件中的三种变体
- 道具的核心形态保持不变，仅改变表面处理方式和色彩方案

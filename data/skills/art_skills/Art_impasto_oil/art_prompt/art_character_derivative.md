# 厚涂油画角色衍生风格提示词

基于 `art_character.md` 中的角色设定，在不同油画技法下的变体。以下每个变体沿用相同的角色描述但采用不同的绘画处理方式。

## 印象派断色角色

```
A figure rendered in soft Impressionist broken color technique. Same character: [inherit description from art_character.md]. Short horizontal dabs of pure pigment placed side by side, allowing optical mixing at a distance. Lavender shadows, Pale Peach highlights, touches of Sage Green in reflected light. Atmospheric haze softens the contours, edges dissolving into the surrounding air. The paint is applied with a light flickering touch—no heavy impasto, no palette knife ridges. Color palette: Cobalt Violet, Pale Rose Madder, Veronese Green, Cerulean Blue. In the tradition of Impressionist portraiture, reminiscent of Renoir's dappled light studies.
```

## 霍克尼式平面丙烯角色

```
A figure rendered in the flat acrylic style of David Hockney's California period. Same character: [inherit description from art_character.md]. Clean, unmodulated color planes with crisp contours—no blending, no chiaroscuro modeling. Turquoise Blue pools of shadow, Terracotta warm tones, Lemon Yellow highlights stark as LA afternoon glare. The paint is applied evenly with no visible brushstroke texture; the surface is deliberately flat, graphic, and poster-like. Strong diagonal composition. Color palette: Turquoise Blue, Terracotta, Lemon Yellow, Emerald Green, Cobalt Blue. In the tradition of mid-century flat acrylic portraiture.
```

## 里维拉式社会现实主义角色变体

```
A figure rendered in the monumental mural style of Diego Rivera. Same character: [inherit description from art_character.md]. Earthy, grounded palette—Burnt Sienna, Yellow Ochre, Terra Rosa, Umber. Simplified volumetric forms with sculptural solidity, figures occupying space with weight and dignity. The paint is applied in broad, deliberate strokes with visible brush texture but not heavy impasto. Golden crucible glow from a low light source wraps the figure in warmth. Background elements suggest labor and industry—tools, machinery, landscapes. Color palette: Burnt Sienna, Yellow Ochre, Terra Rosa, Raw Umber, Chrome Yellow. In the tradition of Mexican muralist social realism.
```

## 混合技法指导

当需要在同一场景中切换/混合上述风格时：
- 从 `art_character.md` 的角色描述中提取核心人物特征（外貌、姿态、服饰）
- 保持角色身份不变，仅改变绘画技法和色彩方案
- 印象派变体：色彩更柔和，边界更模糊，光感更弥散
- 霍克尼变体：色彩更鲜艳饱和，边界更锐利，光影更强烈
- 里维拉变体：色调更质朴厚重，体量感更强，叙事性更突出

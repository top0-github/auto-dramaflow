# 厚涂油画场景提示词

## 风格编号：No. 137（印象派河流）、No. 138（霍克尼泳池）、No. 139（罗斯科色域）

---

## No. 137：印象派河流 · 黄昏

```
A riverside landscape at dusk painted in soft Impressionist broken color. Short horizontal dabs of Lavender, Pale Peach, and Sage Green placed side by side across the canvas surface. The river surface shimmers with flickering reflections—dashes of pale gold and rose catching the last light. Distant trees rendered as hazy masses of violet-green, their forms dissolving into the evening mist. The sky is a graded wash of fading cerulean to warm peach at the horizon. No heavy impasto—the paint is applied with a light touch, each stroke short and distinct, allowing optical mixing at a distance. Canvas weave visible beneath the thin paint layers. In the tradition of Monet's Seine River series, capturing the transient effects of twilight on water.
```

## No. 138：霍克尼泳池 · 正午

```
A mid-century swimming pool scene painted in flat acrylic style after David Hockney. Turquoise Blue water rendered as a single flat plane with schematic ripples—white squiggles indicating surface movement. The pool deck is Terracotta pink, baking under Los Angeles afternoon glare. A Lemon Yellow diving board cuts diagonally across the composition. Clean, hard-edged color shapes—no blending, no atmospheric perspective, no impasto texture. The sky is a flat field of Cobalt Blue, cloudless and intense. Palm tree silhouettes reduced to graphic dark green shapes at the composition's edge. The overall effect is deliberately flat, bright, and graphic—celebrating the artificial paradise of suburban California. Visible canvas weave beneath the acrylic flatness.
```

## No. 139：罗斯科色域 · 抽象

```
An abstract color field composition in the tradition of Mark Rothko. Three stacked rectangular blocks of color float against a deep Oxblood Red ground. The upper rectangle is Burnt Orange, softly glowing with hazy feathered edges that bleed into the surrounding red. The middle rectangle is Ochre, warmer and more opaque at its core, dissolving toward its borders. The lower rectangle is a darker Burnt Umber, anchoring the composition. All edges are deliberately soft and blurred—no hard lines, no geometric precision. The color blocks appear to hover and pulse, creating a meditative, immersive visual experience. Visible canvas texture throughout, the paint applied in thin, translucent washes that build depth through layering. In the tradition of Rothko's Seagram Murals.
```

## 综合场景：油画风景三连画

将上述三种场景并置为三连画（triptych），从具象到抽象的递进：

- **左联**（No. 137）：印象派河流黄昏——具象自然景观
- **中联**（No. 138）：霍克尼泳池正午——半抽象人工景观
- **右联**（No. 139）：罗斯科色域抽象——纯粹色彩冥想

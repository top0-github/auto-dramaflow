# 厚涂油画道具提示词

## 基础提示词模板

```
A [prop] painted in thick impasto oil. Palette knife strokes define [texture details]. Deep shadows in paint ridges. Against a [color] background with visible canvas weave.
```

## 模板参数说明

| 参数 | 说明 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| [prop] | 道具名称 | ceramic vase / wooden chair / brass lantern |
| [texture details] | 道具的材质纹理细节 | the glaze's reflective sheen / the wood grain and carved details |
| [color] | 背景颜色 | Deep Ultramarine / Burnt Sienna / Chrome Yellow |

## 厚涂道具示例

### 陶瓷花瓶

```
A ceramic vase painted in thick impasto oil. Palette knife strokes define the glaze's reflective sheen, the curved body built from sweeping arcs of paint. Deep shadows in paint ridges at the vase's contours and lip. Against a Deep Ultramarine background with visible canvas weave showing through the dark pigment.
```

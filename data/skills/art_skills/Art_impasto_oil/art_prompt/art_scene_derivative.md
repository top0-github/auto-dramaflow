# 厚涂油画场景衍生风格提示词

基于 `art_scene.md` 中的三种场景设定，在其他油画技法下的交叉变体。

## 印象派风景 → 厚涂变体

将 No. 137 的河流黄昏以厚涂技法重新呈现：

```
A riverside landscape at dusk painted in thick impasto oil. Palette knife strokes build the river surface in heavy horizontal ridges—Chrome Yellow and Pale Rose Madder catching the last light. Trees along the bank rendered in sculptural swirls of Viridian Green and Deep Ultramarine, their forms projecting from the canvas as 3D paint masses. The sky is built from sweeping palette knife arcs of fading Lavender to warm Peach. Every inch of the canvas carries expressive turbulent paint movement—no flat areas remain. Deep shadows form in the paint ridges where the palette knife carved the pigment. Canvas texture visible beneath the thickest paint applications. In the tradition of post-impressionist impasto landscape.
```

## 霍克尼泳池 → 厚涂变体

将 No. 138 的平面泳池以厚涂技法重新呈现：

```
A swimming pool scene painted in thick impasto oil rather than flat acrylic. Turquoise Blue water built from rhythmic palette knife strokes forming wave-like ridges across the pool surface. The Terracotta deck is a heavy crust of pigment, palette knife scrapes revealing the canvas weave beneath. Lemon Yellow diving board rendered as a thick projecting wedge of paint. Strong overhead sunlight creates deep shadows within every paint ridge—the impasto texture catches light on its peaks while its valleys plunge into Deep Ultramarine darkness. The flat graphic aesthetic of Hockney is translated into a sculptural impasto language while retaining the California color sensibility. Canvas texture visible.
```

## 罗斯科色域 → 厚涂变体

将 No. 139 的抽象色域以厚涂技法重新呈现：

```
An abstract color field composition executed in thick impasto oil. The floating rectangles are built from heavy palette knife applications—Burnt Orange pigment piled in rhythmic ridges at the upper block, Ochre applied in sweeping dollops at the center, Burnt Umber anchoring the lower field. Unlike Rothko's thin washes, this version celebrates the physical substance of paint itself. The feathered edges are achieved not through blending but through the natural irregularity of palette knife edges. Oxblood Red ground visible between and around the color blocks. Deep shadows in paint ridges create an unexpected sculptural dimension to the meditative color field. Canvas texture visible. In the tradition of abstract expressionist impasto.
```

## 交叉混合指导

| 原场景 | 原技法 | 变体技法 | 核心转换原则 |
|--------|--------|----------|-------------|
| No. 137 河流 | 印象派断色 | 厚涂 | 横向短笔触 → 横向调色刀脊线；视觉混合 → 物理浮雕 |
| No. 138 泳池 | 平面丙烯 | 厚涂 | 平坦色块 → 立体颜料堆叠；图形轮廓 → 刀痕边界 |
| No. 139 色域 | 薄涂色域 | 厚涂 | 透明薄层 → 厚重不透明层；羽化晕染 → 刀缘自然过渡 |

注意：逆向转换（从厚涂到平面或印象派）也适用于此框架，只需反向操作上述转换原则。

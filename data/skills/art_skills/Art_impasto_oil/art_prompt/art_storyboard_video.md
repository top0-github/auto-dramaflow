# 厚涂油画视频分镜提示词

## 视频风格定义

后印象派油画动画，可见的调色刀纹理，立体厚涂笔触，画布经纬纹理可见。颜料沿笔触方向缓慢流动和呼吸（如梵高《星月夜》中的漩涡运动）。

## 视频基础提示词模板

```
Post-impressionist oil painting animation. Visible palette knife texture, impasto 3D paint strokes, canvas weave visible. Motion: paint slowly flowing and breathing along brushstroke direction, swirling like Van Gogh's Starry Night sky. Color palette: Chrome Yellow, Deep Ultramarine, Vermilion Red, Viridian Green, Burnt Sienna.
```

## 分镜时间线

### 镜头 01 | 0:00–0:05 | 建立镜头 · 画布近景

```
Tight macro close-up on canvas surface. Visible weave of linen canvas, thick impasto paint ridges catching warm light. A palette knife enters the frame, pushing a heavy dollop of Chrome Yellow pigment across the surface. The paint spreads in rhythmic ridges, shadows forming in the valleys between strokes. Slow, deliberate camera movement tracking the knife's path. No digital smoothness—every motion honors the physical resistance of oil paint. Color: Chrome Yellow against Deep Ultramarine ground.
```

### 镜头 02 | 0:05–0:12 | 颜料呼吸 · 天空形成

```
Wide shot of the canvas surface as paint begins to move on its own. Swirling strokes of Deep Ultramarine and Viridian Green slowly flow and breathe along their original brushstroke direction, like Van Gogh's Starry Night sky in slow motion. The paint ridges pulse gently—expanding and contracting with a living rhythm. Whorls and spirals form organically in the pigment. Chrome Yellow stars flicker into existence as thick daubs of paint that pulse with warm light. Canvas weave visible throughout.
```

### 镜头 03 | 0:12–0:20 | 角色浮现 · 厚涂人物

```
A figure gradually emerges from the swirling paint field. Palette knife strokes coalesce into facial features, clothing folds, gesturing hands—all rendered in heavy impasto. Vermilion Red accents appear in the figure's garments. The figure moves with the same slow breathing rhythm as the background, the paint of their form flowing gently along the contours of their body. Harsh overhead light creates deep shadows within the paint ridges. Close-up on the face as individual palette knife strokes define the features.
```

### 镜头 04 | 0:20–0:30 | 道具与细节 · 雕塑感物件

```
Camera pans across impasto-rendered props and environmental details. A vase built from sweeping arcs of Viridian Green paint. A table surface defined by horizontal chrome yellow knife strokes. Each object occupies real three-dimensional space on the canvas surface—the paint physically projects from the picture plane. Deep shadows in paint ridges give each object weight and presence. The paint continues its slow breathing motion throughout.
```

### 镜头 05 | 0:30–0:40 | 场景展开 · 风景全景

```
Wide establishing shot of the full oil-painted landscape. The river rendered in horizontal impasto ridges catching pale light. Trees as sculptural swirls of Viridian Green and Deep Ultramarine. The sky breathes and flows with slow chromatic movement—Lavender dissolving into Pale Peach at the horizon. Camera drifts slowly across the canvas surface, revealing the topography of paint ridges and valleys. Canvas weave visible beneath thinner paint areas.
```

### 镜头 06 | 0:40–0:48 | 色彩冥想 · 罗斯科段落

```
Transition to abstract color field. Oxblood Red ground fills the frame. Burnt Orange and Ochre rectangles slowly emerge, their feathered edges pulsing and breathing. The color blocks hover and shift subtly, creating a meditative visual rhythm. Paint texture becomes the primary subject—the physical substance of pigment celebrated in extreme close-up. Slow dissolve between color fields.
```

### 镜头 07 | 0:48–0:55 | 回归与收束

```
Return to the original canvas macro shot. The palette knife completes its stroke and lifts away. The finished painting breathes gently—all elements in harmonious slow motion. Chrome Yellow highlights catch the light one final time. Fade to the canvas texture alone—linen weave, paint ridges, the quiet presence of the physical painting surface.
```

## 运动原则

| 元素 | 运动方式 | 参考 |
|------|---------|------|
| 天空/背景 | 沿笔触方向缓慢漩涡流动 | 梵高《星月夜》 |
| 人物 | 沿身体轮廓缓慢呼吸式流动 | 颜料浮雕的潮汐式起伏 |
| 道具 | 微弱的立体脉动，保持雕塑感 | 颜料堆叠的缓慢沉降 |
| 阴影 | 随颜料脊线起伏而加深/变浅 | 浮雕光影的自然变化 |
| 色彩过渡 | 颜料粒子级的缓慢色移 | 断色技法的动态光学混合 |

## 禁止项

- 禁止数字化的平滑过渡或CGI混合
- 禁止超出笔触方向的颜料流动
- 禁止丢失画布纹理
- 禁止使用数字合成式的图层效果
- 禁止超出五种核心颜料（Chrome Yellow, Deep Ultramarine, Vermilion Red, Viridian Green, Burnt Sienna）的额外颜色
